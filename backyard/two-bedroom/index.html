Make room (or two)
for more.

Your Backyard, your rules. Use the secondary bedroom as a luxe, cozy living room...

...or max out wardrobe storage to accommodate long-term guests.

The secondary bedroom is separated by a 4' pocket door, allowing you to easily divide Backyard for work, play, or as a place to stay.

Our spacious bathroom includes a 30" × 60" walk-in shower, extra-wide vanity, washer and dryer and plenty of additional storage for supplies.

Call it the ultimate flex: because a second bedroom isn’t just for sleeping, it’s for dreaming bigger, too.
