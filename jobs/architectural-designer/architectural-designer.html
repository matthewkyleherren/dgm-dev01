Architectural Designer

DGM is looking for a talented and highly motivated Architectural Designer to join the Product & Design Team as an inspirational force for creating new ways of living. As an integral part of DGM’s design process, you will contribute significantly to the development of new architectural products as well as design services for multiunit projects & proposals. Your keen eye for detail, excellent design skills, and ability to visualize and communicate ideas effectively will be essential to our success.

Responsibilities

  • Actively participate in the conceptualization and development of new product design work, focusing on creating a body of bold, beautiful, and delightful works of architecture for DGM’s building system.
  • Proactively iterate on design concepts, exploring various approaches to refine and optimize design solutions at both the macro and micro scale.
  • Identify and resolve design challenges, offering creative solutions that turn constraints into opportunities, preserving a balance of functionality, aesthetics, and feasibility.
  • Produce high-quality 2D and 3D representations, including renderings, diagrams, and models, to effectively communicate design intent to both internal and external stakeholders.
  • Utilize industry-standard design softwares (Rhino,etc) to create accurate and detailed architectural drawings, representations, and documentation.
  • Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams, including brand, GTM, sales, development, and engineering to ensure seamless integration of product & design solutions.
  • Execute quality content creation for product & design initiatives, sales collateral, and developer projects.
  • Manage design communication with external visualization partners by preparing necessary design documentation and providing productive feedback for final asset completion.
  • Product & Design research: Stay refreshed on innovative thinking around new forms of living by initiating research on historical housing typologies/case studies and surveying contemporary practices.
  • Helm the organization of design libraries, systems, and processes to streamline incorporation of product assets, materials, and specifications.
  • Assist in prototyping product visuals for new GTM strategies alongside Brand and Sales teams.

Qualifications

  • Professional degree in Architecture required.
  • 3-8 years of proven work experience in architectural design discipline
  • Experience in modular or residential construction is a plus.
  • A portfolio demonstrating strong design skills and projects that resonate with DGM’s ambitions
  • Prolific proficiency in presentation, modeling, and visualization softwares
  • Deep appreciation of aesthetics and healthy obsession for what makes good space.
  • A passion for housing and designing around the rituals of living.
  • Be dexterous and adaptable to changing priorities.
  • Excellent understanding of architectural principles, materials, and construction methods.
  • Excellent communication skills. English proficiency required.
  • Experience in driving design presentations & asset production for short RFP submissions.
  • Experience in collaborating and managing consultants in order to meet program milestones.
  • Partner with Sales, Brand, and Engineering teams to enable the rollout of new products.
  • Proficiency in prototyping new ideas with rapid and refined fabrication methods leveraging our in house Prototyping shop and Factory teams.
  • Solid attention to detail, highly entrepreneurial, creative, open-minded, persistent, team-first mentality, and highly collaborative.
  • Keenness to grow and develop professionally within an early-stage startup environment
  • Outstanding organizational and prioritization skills, with the ability to manage multiple projects in a fast-paced environment and consistently deliver on key milestones.

Benefits

  • Competitive base salary of $110-130k/year dependent on experience with an additional yearly target bonus.
  • Hybrid work schedule with 3 days each week in our Redwood City office.
  • Early stage employee equity.
  • Exceptional health, dental, and vision insurance.
  • 401k eligibility after 6 months.
  • Flexible PTO policy.
