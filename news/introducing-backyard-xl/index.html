Introducing Backyard XL 8

Above: Backyard XL 8 includes two bedrooms and two baths in 800 square feet.
Download image

We’re proud to unveil Backyard XL 8, our latest ADU model that makes it easy for homeowners to add more flexible living space to their existing properties. With two bedrooms and two bathrooms, the luxe 800-square-foot home provides ample space with larger appliances, and premium amenities.

Backyard XL 8 is designed for comfort and functionality, offering a blank canvas for whatever you may need—be it a guesthouse, home office, or rental unit. The new unit includes:

  • An open, airy living room and kitchen ideal for entertaining
  • A suite of full-size electric appliances, including a next-gen induction cooktop and panelized dishwasher
  • A complete laundry work area with a stacked washer and dryer, plus storage for supplies
  • Spacious bedrooms with built-in wardrobes
  • Sanctuary-like en suite and shared bathrooms with storage
  • Customizable double doors and Western red cedar decks
Above: Backyard XL 8's open-plan living room and kitchen.
Download image

Mike McNamara, DGM co-founder and CEO, said:

“Like all of our units, Backyard XL 8 is designed, engineered, delivered and installed by DGM. Thanks to our competitive financing option, we’ve opened up the opportunity for millions of homeowners in California and beyond to realize the full potential of their homes.”

As ADUs continue to skyrocket in popularity—1 in 5 houses built in California last year were ADUs—we’re committed to building more units in a way that helps people lead fuller lives.

To learn more about why Backyard XL 8 is DGM’s most-requested unit, visit our website.

About DGM

DGM is on a mission to transform the way we live, starting with the home. The company’s first product, Backyard, is a high-quality accessory dwelling unit (ADU) that easily adds additional living space to your property. Backyard is designed to be flexible, giving homeowners more room for whatever they need—be it an in-law suite, home office, or rental unit. DGM uniquely manages Backyard from concept to completion, handling everything from product design and manufacturing to permitting, delivery, installation, and financing.

Started by CEO Mike McNamara and Airbnb co-founder Joe Gebbia, DGM is backed by Airbnb, Thrive Capital, 8VC and other leading corporate and venture investors. The company is headquartered in Redwood City, CA and owns a manufacturing facility in Mexicali, MX.
